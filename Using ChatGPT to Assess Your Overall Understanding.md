        Using ChatGPT to Assess Your Overall Understanding
    </h1>
    <p style="font-size:16px; font-family:Georgia, serif; line-height: 1.5em; text-indent: 20px; color:#333;">
        ChatGPT can be a valuable tool for assessing your understanding of programming concepts. By engaging in discussions about various topics, you can identify areas where your knowledge is strong and areas that need more work.
    </p>
    <p style="font-size:16px; font-family:Georgia, serif; line-height: 1.5em; text-indent: 20px; color:#333;">
        Here are some ways to use ChatGPT for self-assessment:
    </p>
    <ul style="font-size:16px; font-family:Georgia, serif; color:#333; line-height: 1.5em; margin-left: 40px;">
        <li><strong>Explain concepts to ChatGPT:</strong> Try explaining programming concepts to ChatGPT as if you were teaching someone else. The AI can provide feedback on your explanation and fill in any gaps.</li>
        <li><strong>Answer practice questions:</strong> Ask ChatGPT to generate practice questions about specific topics, then try to answer them. You can then check your answers with the AI.</li>
        <li><strong>Code review practice:</strong> Share your code with ChatGPT and ask for a review. This can help you understand if you’re applying concepts correctly in your coding.</li>
        <li><strong>Solve hypothetical scenarios:</strong> Ask ChatGPT to present you with hypothetical programming scenarios and explain how you would approach them.</li>
    </ul>
